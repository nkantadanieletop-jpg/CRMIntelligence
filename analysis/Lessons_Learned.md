# Lessons Learned

## Project Reflection

This project simulated the work of a commercial data analyst supporting a B2B SaaS sales organization.

Rather than focusing only on writing SQL queries, the project emphasized understanding business problems, translating them into analytical questions, and communicating findings that support decision-making.

---

## Technical Skills Strengthened

- SQL data analysis
- Common Table Expressions (CTEs)
- Window functions
- Revenue and sales funnel analysis
- KPI design
- Data modeling for Power BI
- Business documentation

---

## Business Insights

This project reinforced several important commercial analytics principles:

- Pipeline growth does not always translate into revenue growth.
- High-quality lead sources often outperform high-volume lead sources.
- Opportunity aging is a leading indicator of forecast risk.
- Effective analytics combines technical analysis with business storytelling.

---

## Future Improvements

Potential enhancements include:

- Interactive Power BI dashboard (.pbix)
- Additional sales forecasting models
- Customer retention analysis
- Predictive win probability scoring
- Automated ETL pipeline
- Live CRM data integration

---

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates an end-to-end commercial analytics workflow, from data modeling and SQL analysis to executive reporting and business recommendations.

The objective was not simply to analyze data, but to produce insights that support better commercial decision-making.
