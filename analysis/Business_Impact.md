# Business Impact Assessment

## Executive Perspective

The analysis identified several commercial inefficiencies affecting revenue generation and sales productivity.

Although pipeline volume remains healthy, revenue realization is constrained by operational bottlenecks throughout the sales process.

---

## Revenue Leakage

Major contributors include:

- Opportunities remaining inactive for extended periods.
- Low proposal-to-negotiation conversion.
- High-value enterprise opportunities with prolonged sales cycles.
- Forecasted deals failing to close within expected timelines.

These issues collectively reduce predictable revenue generation.

---

## Sales Productivity

Sales representatives spend considerable effort pursuing opportunities with relatively low conversion probability.

Improving lead qualification and pipeline prioritization would increase overall sales efficiency.

---

## Marketing ROI

Referral channels consistently outperform outbound acquisition.

Increasing investment in high-performing acquisition channels would improve customer acquisition efficiency while lowering cost per opportunity.

---

## Operational Recommendations

- Introduce pipeline aging alerts.
- Review proposal follow-up processes.
- Improve sales forecasting discipline.
- Prioritize enterprise opportunities based on probability and deal value.
- Allocate more resources toward referral acquisition.
